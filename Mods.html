Hobbies

As a person with many ambitions, I value the free time I get. I enjoy reading books (both fiction and non-fiction) and analysing them. I ride my bike almost everyday and I consider it one of the best means of transportation. Furthermore, beside my competitive sports career, I enjoy shooting hoops and playing with my friends in my neighbourhood. Last but not least, I enjoy photography and any kind of art in general. When abroad, I like to spend my time in museums as well as strolling in tiny streets. My photographs mainly incorporate nature, however I do enjoy taking pictures of infrastructure and people that I find interesting along my walks.
